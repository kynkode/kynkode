MY PET
DOCUMENTS

Keeping your pet's documents organized is vital for both medical urgency and international travel compliance. Centralized digital records ensure your companion's safety and smooth passage across borders, providing instant accessibility when it matters most.

Global Travel Readiness

MANDATORY

Pet Passport

Vaccination Book: Pet & Owner Details, Microchip Sticker, Vaccination Records, Health Record with Deworming Proof and Flea & Tick Treatment, Medical Notes.

ESSENTIAL

Medical Records

Documented history of your pet's health, including veterinary consultations, diagnoses, vaccinations, deworming, medications, surgeries, laboratory results, imaging, allergies, chronic conditions, weight records, and other treatments or medical events throughout the pet's lifetime.

REGULATORY

Import/Export Permits

Special government authorizations required by specific countries to manage the movement of animals through safe corridors.

LOGISTICAL

Airlines Forms

Carrier-specific documentation focusing on pet health and container compliance for cabin or cargo transport safety.

Life-Cycle Medical Logs

  • Pet medical records act as a life-cycle log, capturing every crucial step of your pet's health journey. This includes a detailed vaccination history to protect against local and regional diseases, ensuring your pet is always current on mandated shots.
  • Accurate prescriptions and chronic condition notes are vital for emergency care or when visiting new clinics. Having these ready-to-access ensures that medical professionals can make informed decisions based on existing treatment plans and specific health requirements.
  • Comprehensive lab results and diagnostic reports provide a clinical baseline for your pet. Keeping these digitized records facilitates seamless handovers during referral visits and provides peace of mind that no medical detail is left to memory when it matters most.
