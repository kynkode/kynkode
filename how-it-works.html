How
Trackyn
Works

Trackyn connects a pet’s microchip to owner details in a secure database so lost pets can be quickly identified and reunited.

To update your details or transfer ownership: If you are a registered user and need to update your contact information or your pet’s details, please press the 'Update Details/Transfer Ownership' button. After Registration, please wait for your pet’s unique QR tag and Digital ID.

Register Your Pet: Detailed Steps

Step 1: Enter Your Contact Information

  • First Name & Last Name: Please use your full legal name for authentication.
  • Email: Provide a valid email address to receive secure Trackyn alerts and search reports.
  • Phone: Your primary contact number ensures we can reach you quickly in case of recovery.

Step 2: Enter Your Pet’s Details

  • Pet Name: Your pet's name as it should appear on their verified digital ID.
  • Microchip Number: Must be exactly 15 digits. Ensure there are no spaces or letters; the app will register an error if the count is incorrect.
  • Pet Gender: Select between Male, Female, or Unknown/Other.
  • Description: Detail your pet's breed, primary colors, and any distinct physical markings for visual identification.

Step 3: Upload Required Photos

  • Microchip Sticker Photo: Upload a sharp, clear picture of the barcode sticker found in your pet's papers.
  • Pet Photo: Provide a clear, recent, full-body photo of your pet to aid in visual recognition during recovery.

Step 4: Confirm Payment

Check the "Paid" box to verify your transaction. Note that the registration mainframe will not allow form submission if this box remains unchecked.

Step 5: Submit Your Registration

Click "REGISTER MY PET" to finalize. You will see "UPLOADING TO TRACKYN...". Please do not refresh. Once completed, you will see "Thanks for your registration" and have the option to add another pet.

Step 2: Your Pet Joins the Network

Once registered, your pet’s unique microchip ID is stored securely within the Trackyn mainframe. This digital identifier is available 24/7 to our vast network of veterinarians, animal shelters, and recovery partners, ensuring your pet is instantly recognizable across the sector.

Step 3: If Your Pet Is Lost

1.

A microchip scanner is used by a veterinarian or rescuer when your lost pet is found.

2.

The unique ID is checked against the Trackyn database for rapid identification.

3.

Trackyn instantly receives request for identification and determines the rescuer's position.

4.

You are alerted, ensuring a seamless and fast reunion.

How Microchip Search & Verification Works

1. Enter the Microchip Number

Where to find the 15-digit number (papers, scanner, Trackyn QR). The search field requires exactly 15 digits and won’t search otherwise.

2. The Verification Process

If location is not automatically picked, please wait for about 20 seconds for location telemetry to sync. If it does not sync automatically before 20 seconds are over, kindly input the location in latitude-longitude format. Click HERE for help.

3. Digital ID Card

Contains: pet photo slot, name, owner, gender, description, and "Verified Resident" badge.

4. Post-Search Actions

  • Download Digital ID – generates a high-quality image of the ID card.
  • Download QR Code – creates a unique QR code for the pet’s tag.
  • I Found This Pet – triggers a high-accuracy GPS search (within about 15km) to find nearby vets or shelters. Please wait for about 20 seconds for location telemetry to sync. If it does not, kindly input the location in latitude-longitude format. Click HERE for help.

Quick Tip: If location sync fails after waiting ~20 seconds, a manual input box will appear where you can paste coordinates from Google Maps so recovery telemetry is still sent to the owner.
